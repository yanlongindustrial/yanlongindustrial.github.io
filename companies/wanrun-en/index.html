WanRun

Jiangsu Wanrun Components of Automobiles Co., Ltd is special for press parts. It locates in Kunshan, Province Jiangsu. The total investment is over 20 M RMB. With over 10 Presses, one 3D Measure machine and 2 press lines, the company has a yearly capacity of 6,336 M strokes of the presses over 800 t and 22 M strokes for the small presses. Under ISO/TS 16959:2008 for the quality management automobile industry the company has become A-Class supplier for the SAIC Volkswagen with 52 employees since 2013. Adhering to the philosophy of “management integrity, quality observing, customer satisfaction and sustainable development”, the company is striving for first-class brand.

Our main customers including SVW,SGM and Nanjing Auto Etc.
