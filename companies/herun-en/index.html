HeRun

Jiangsu Hyrun Auto Die Co., Ltd is registered in Kunshan Jiangsu in April 2010, purchases almost 37mu land, RMB 0.12 billion for investment in Stage I to build the scale of equivalent 260 sets of dies each year.

The company covers the land of 24304 square meters and workshop area of 12780 square meters. There are 15 sets of CNC, 8 sets of try out press and almost 30 sets of small and middle auxiliary equipments. Major products: manufacture of large sedan’s inner and outer cover parts and so on.

Company upholds the spirit of “selfless dedication, the pursuit of excellence” spirit of enterprise, “to create first-class professional automotive body mold business” as the goal, the company on October 12, 2012 passed ISO9001: 2008 quality system certification, and in early February 2013 was SAIC-Volkswagen rated A2 * mold suppliers, FAW-Volkswagen A-class mold manufacturing suppliers, SAIC passenger car excellent cooperation suppliers,Jiangsu Province High-Tech Enterprise,Shanghai Gold-Brand Mold Enterprise Award .

Jiangsu Herun Auto Body Mould Co., Ltd. has become the core supplier of molds such as FAW-Volkswagen, SAIC Volkswagen, Shanghai Auto Side, Fender, and Back Cover, as well as many overseas customers such as Gestamp and ThyssenKrupp. Make molds such as side panels, fenders, top covers, and back covers.
