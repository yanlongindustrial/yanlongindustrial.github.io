YanLong Industrial

Shanghai YanLong Industry Co., Ltd. was found in Aug. 2009, which is originated from Hebei Xinlin Car Body & Die Co., Ltd. in since 1986. As its key business YanLong specializes in designing and manufacturing dies, gauges and fixtures for inner and outer plate of car body as well as for manufacturing press parts and sub-assembly car body.

Now the location of its headquarter is in town Anting of Jiading District in the city of Shanghai. With the investment totally of 950 Mil. RMB, the sales in 2017 of 850 Mil. RMB and the staff of 1372, YanLong has established 5 filial under itself: Chengdu Jiarun Components of Automobiles Co., Ltd. (2009), Jiangsu Hyrun Auto Body Die Co., Ltd.(2010), Jiangsu Wanrun Components of Automobiles Co., Ltd.(2010) and Chengdu Shirun Components of Automobiles Co., Ltd. (2017) as well as Changshu Xingrun Auto Parts Co., Ltd.(2019).

Jiarun, Hyrun, Wanrun, Shirun and “Xing”run in planning, mean “Jia He Wan Shi Xin” (Family in Harmony bring everything prosperous). Since 10 years after establishment, YanLong has been developing from a die and press parts supplier for the local superior car manufacturers to a benchmark for the national brand with extension of relevant industry branch, and realizing its own development goals step by step. The new target set up by YanLong itself is to become a preferred partner for car manufacturers both local and abroad to share the achievement of automobile industry in China.

YanLong Industry possesses standard workshops covering an area of over 127000 m2 totally, the workshops areas are totally 85000 m2 large. There are 8 mechanical and hydraulic press lines in a range of 800 to 1600 t, 2 car body welding lines, 15 CNC machines and other relevant facilities such as 3D measure machine, laser welding. YanLong has over hundred technicians with bachelor and master degree， all staff for quality insurance are well qualified. With these YanLong has the capacity of designing and manufacturing dies for large car body outer panel, manufacturing car body prototype and developing dies with innovation and application continuously. YanLong Industry has get certified with ISO/TS 16949 and ISO 9001 since 2011 and 2012.

“Profession, Care, Benefit”. The core values of YanLong bring customers professional, effective and careful service. Under this philosophy YanLong has established long-term business relationship with local automobile leading manufacturer such as SAIC Volkswagen, FAW Volkswagen, furthermore other carmakers like SAIC Motor, Changan Ford, Cherry, Volvo, Magna, Gestamp, at the same time also long-term good cooperation with the automobile suppliers such as ThyssenKrupp. YanLong has formed long-term strategic partnership with the 2 JVs of Volkswagen in south and north China, is the A2 class supplier for SAIC VW and A class supplier for FAW VW. Meanwhile, YanLong is also the excellent supplier for SAIC Motor. In addition, YanLong has been awarded “High-Tech Enterprise of Jiangsu” and “Gold Brand Automobile Die Firm of Shanghai” by Shanghai Die Technical Association (2017).

Facing the global technical development and market competition, YanLong will still make great effort on improving continuously, striving for excellence to become a preferred partner for the carmakers.
